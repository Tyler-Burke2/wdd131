Overview

Purpose

The purpose of this site is to provide future employers with additional information about me beyond just what my resume presents. When applying for jobs on campus at BYU-Idaho, they often require a link to a linkedin OR a personal site. The creation of this site provides me a more personal webpage for employers to look at.

Audience

This website is geared towards employers for any position which I may apply to in the future.

Dynamic elements

The main page of the site will be a general "About me" page in regards to necessary information for employers. It will be dynamic by including a timeline of milestones from my life that only appear as the user scrolls as well as a button at the bottom to generate random fun facts about myself. The child page of the site will be focused on my resume. It will be dynamic by including a dropdown menu which will allow a filter to show additional information beyond just what the resume presents.
